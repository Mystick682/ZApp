Common Questions

How do I fund my wallet?

Click "Fund Wallet" in the sidebar, transfer the displayed amount to the account shown, fill the form and tap Notify Admin. Your wallet is credited after verification.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. All payments are processed securely. We never store your card or account details.

My data didn't deliver. What do I do?

Wait a few minutes — most orders deliver under 30 seconds but network delays can occur. If it takes more than 10 minutes, contact us via WhatsApp with your transaction ID.

How do I change my transaction PIN?

Go to Settings → Transaction PIN section, enter your new 4-digit PIN and tap Update PIN.

Can I get a refund?

Failed transactions are automatically reversed to your wallet. For manual funding issues, contact support with your receipt and we'll resolve it within 24 hours.
